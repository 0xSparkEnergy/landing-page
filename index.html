Who we are

Spark Energy Token is a purpose driven token aimed at creating a cleaner environment through the promotion of clean energy and electric vehicles payment solutions.

What we do

Our Services

EV Charging Payment

The Spark Energy Token comes inbuilt with the functionality that enables easy payments for Electric Vehicle (EV) charging, through our dedicated mobile app. We will incorporate partner charging stations across the world.

EV Mileage Rewards

With our mobile app, drivers will be able to earn, without a cap, the Spark Energy Token. This mean there is no limit to what drivers can earn when they use our token to settle their EV charging and transportation needs.

Power Wall Rewards

Modern homes come equipped with effective Inverters to satisfy in-home energy needs. Spark Energy wants to encourage this development. For every user of Powerwalls and Inverters connected to our system through our app, there will be rewards in Spark Energy Tokens(SET).
